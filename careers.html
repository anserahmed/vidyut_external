What We Value

Put Customers First

We always start by understanding our customers' needs and work backward to build solutions. Customer trust is paramount, and we are obsessed with earning and keeping it.

Thinking Data First

We don't rely on gut feelings. We make decisions based on data and insights. Whether numbers or anecdotal evidence, we ensure both align before drawing conclusions.

Always Think Big

We don't settle for small wins. We think long-term and at scale. Every decision we make must contribute to building a market-defining EV ownership ecosystem.

Do More with Less

We value efficiency and resourcefulness. Great outcomes don't always require unlimited resources. They require smart execution.

Disagree and Commit

We encourage debate, independent thinking, and pushing back when needed. However, once a decision is made, we commit wholeheartedly to executing it.

Act like owners

Ownership means taking complete responsibility for your work, decisions, and outcomes. It is about proactively solving problems rather than waiting for instructions. Owners don't just execute tasks. They drive impact and ensure success.

Bias for Action

We move fast and take calculated risks. Not every decision needs weeks of analysis. Sometimes, speed matters more than perfection.

Raising the Bar

We constantly challenge ourselves to be better. Excellence is a moving target, and we are committed to continuous improvement.

Integrity

We value truth and integrity above all else. It is an unwavering commitment to truth seeking and acknowledging and stating facts as they are, even when they challenge personal beliefs, assumptions, or preferences.

SAY / DO = 1

At Vidyut, what we commit to is what we deliver, with no exceptions. Our words and actions must always be in sync. If we say we'll do something, we follow through fully and on time. If circumstances change, we communicate transparently and take responsibility.
