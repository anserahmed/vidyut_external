The Ownership Problem

EV demand is growing. Ownership journey is broken.

Conventional repayment structures do not reflect real-world EV utilization and cash-flow cycles.

Opaque Resale Markets

Without transparent, data-backed resale pathways, long-term value remains uncertain at ownership exit.

Service gaps and inconsistent maintenance support reduce uptime and directly affect earning potential.
